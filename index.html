About Me

I'm Christopher Banda, a passionate full stack developer with over four years of experience building modern web applications that are both beautiful and functional. My journey into web development began during my computer science studies, where I discovered the incredible power of turning ideas into interactive digital experiences.

My expertise spans across frontend technologies like HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, as well as backend development with Node.js and database management. I have a deep appreciation for clean code, intuitive user interfaces, and accessible design principles that ensure everyone can use what I build.

Beyond writing code, I'm passionate about understanding users — their needs, their frustrations, and their goals. This human-centered approach shapes every project I take on. I believe that the best software solves real problems elegantly, without unnecessary complexity. When I'm not coding, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, mentoring junior developers, and exploring the latest trends in web technology. I'm always seeking new opportunities to grow, collaborate, and create meaningful digital products that genuinely make a difference in people's lives.
